## THE PROBLEM

- **74%** of resumes now contain AI-generated content. *(Resume Genius, 2025)*
- **34%** of recruiter time is spent filtering deceptive or unqualified
  candidates. *(iHire, 2025)*
- **$17K** average cost of a bad hire that fails within 90 days. *(SHRM)*
- **65%** of hiring managers have caught candidates using deceptive AI.
- **25%** of candidate interactions projected to involve AI deception by 2028.

---

## THE PRODUCT — Paladin Veritai
Four capabilities:

1. **Pre-Call Skills Gap** — Provide a LinkedIn URL, resume, and job description;
   Paladin produces a skills-gap analysis before you dial.
2. **Live Prompts** — Targeted questions pushed to your screen in real time during
   the call.
3. **Real-Time Context** — Technical terms and jargon defined on-screen as the
   candidate uses them.
4. **Post-Call Summary** — A client-ready candidate summary with confidence scores
   for skills match and culture fit.

## HOW IT WORKS
1. **Prepare** — Load the candidate's materials.
2. **Connect** — Paladin rings your phone, then dials the candidate.
3. **Intelligence** — It listens to both sides and feeds the Intelligence Pane.
4. **Deliver** — You get a client-ready summary.
