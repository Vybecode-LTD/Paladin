**Headline:** Ashford & Briggs Launches Paladin — Real-Time AI Intelligence for Recruiting Calls

**Dateline:** Jacksonville, FL — 2026

**Body:**
Ashford & Briggs today announced the launch of Paladin, a real-time AI intelligence
platform built for recruiters. Unlike tools that record calls for later review or
filter candidates before a conversation ever happens, Paladin works live — during
the phone call — surfacing skills gaps, defining technical jargon in real time, and
pushing targeted follow-up questions to the recruiter's screen.

Paladin runs through a recruiter's existing phone with no app to install. It rings
the recruiter, dials the candidate, listens to both sides of the conversation, and
produces a client-ready candidate summary with confidence scores for skills match
and culture fit.

"In a market flooded with AI-generated resumes and automated screening, the human
conversation is more valuable than ever," said the founders. "Paladin is built to
make that conversation sharper — not to replace it."

The company cites industry data showing 74% of resumes now contain AI-generated
content and that recruiters spend 34% of their time filtering deceptive or
unqualified candidates.
