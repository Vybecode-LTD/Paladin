## The story
Recruiting changed the day generative AI put a polished résumé within everyone's
reach. Overnight, the document that recruiters had relied on for decades stopped
being a trustworthy signal. The industry's response was to add more automation —
more filters, more async screens, more ways to avoid talking to people.

We think that's backwards. When every written signal can be faked, the live
conversation becomes *more* valuable, not less. So we built Paladin to make that
conversation as sharp as it can possibly be: real-time intelligence that rides
along on the call and hands the recruiter better information at exactly the moment
they need it.

## Mission
To keep the human at the center of hiring — and give that human superpowers.
