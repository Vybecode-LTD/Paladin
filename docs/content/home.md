## Product overview (4 pillars, condensed for home)
**Section title:** Intelligence that rides along on the call.

1. **Pre-Call Skills Gap** — Drop in a LinkedIn URL, résumé, and job description.
   Walk into the call already knowing where to dig.
2. **Live Prompts** — The right follow-up question appears on your screen the
   moment it's relevant.
3. **Real-Time Context** — Unfamiliar tech or jargon is defined on-screen as the
   candidate says it. Never bluff through a technical answer again.
4. **Post-Call Summary** — A client-ready write-up with confidence scores for
   skills match and culture fit, seconds after you hang up.
