## The flow

### Step 1 — Prepare
Load the candidate's materials into Paladin: LinkedIn URL, résumé, and the job
description. In seconds, you get a pre-call skills-gap read that tells you exactly
where to focus. *Optional but powerful — even a cold call benefits from live
prompts and context.*

### Step 2 — Connect
Paladin rings your phone first. You answer, and it dials the candidate and bridges
the call. To the candidate, it's a normal phone call from you — because it is.
Nothing to install, nothing to explain.

### Step 3 — Intelligence
As you talk, Paladin listens to both sides and feeds the Intelligence Pane on your
screen: a live transcript, timed follow-up prompts, on-the-fly definitions for
technical terms, and markers on moments worth revisiting. You glance, you steer,
you stay fully present in the conversation.

### Step 4 — Deliver
The moment you hang up, Paladin generates a client-ready summary — key points,
skills-match confidence, culture-fit read — ready to forward to your hiring
manager or client. The call is captured while it's fresh, without you writing a
word.
